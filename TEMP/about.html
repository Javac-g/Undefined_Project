    Our Minimum Requirement for Domain Marketing

    Your desired domain name must consist of no more than two descriptive words.

    We currently support domain purchases primarily for standard extensions (.com, .net, .org). Special TLDs (.io, .ai, etc.) may require separate approval.

    You agree to allow us to act as your exclusive broker for the requested domain purchase.

    You agree not to engage with other services, brokers, or platforms for the same domain while we are negotiating on your behalf.

    You must notify us immediately if you have already had any contact, correspondence, or offers regarding the domain.

    We do not engage in negotiations where prior commitments exist unless fully disclosed.

    Domains containing spelling errors, typographical mistakes, or terms violating intellectual property rights (trademarks, brands) will not be accepted.

    We reserve the right to decline requests that are unlawful, offensive, or misleading.

    If applicable, you must provide any available traffic or ownership history related to the domain.

    Full prepayment is required before we begin domain negotiations or registration on your behalf.

    Payment methods must comply with anti-fraud policies; suspicious transactions will be rejected without exception.

    Basic verification (email and/or phone) is required before transaction processing.

    We reserve the right to request additional identification in case of high-value or flagged transactions.

    By proceeding with domain brokerage, you accept that completed purchases and domain transfers are final and non-refundable.

    Attempting chargebacks after successful domain delivery will result in blacklisting and possible legal action.

    You confirm that you are eligible under your local laws to purchase and own domain names.

    We are not responsible for any legal restrictions or penalties in your jurisdiction.
