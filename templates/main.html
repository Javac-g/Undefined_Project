FAQ

What payment methods can I use?

Shodo accepts credit and debit card payments.
You can pay with VISA, MasterCard, American Express, and JCB.

For API plans and enterprise plans, payment can be made by invoice or bank transfer.

Do you support payment by invoice?

Bank transfers are only available for API plans and Enterprise plans.

For Premium and Business plans, we require payment by credit card.

Are security measures in place (regarding payments, transactions, other information communications, and data storage)?

Yes. For payments, we use Stripe (https://stripe.com/), a payment service used by major Japanese companies.
Shodo does not handle credit card information, and payments are made securely between you and Stripe.

Shodo itself has been developed by cloud technology experts with security in mind.
Data entrusted to us by our customers is stored in an encrypted database that cannot be accessed from the outside.
All communications use secure communications called HTTPS, and the servers and log information used for communications are also operated in a cloud that cannot be accessed from the outside.
In addition, the vulnerability of the server is checked regularly to ensure that it is not infected with malware or ransomware.
Payment and data on the Shodo cloud are only accessible by designated staff, and two-step authentication is mandatory when logging in.

Is the server dedicated to each contract?

No, Shodo's servers are used by all Shodo customers.
Shodo's web app and proofreading API are operated on a multi-tenant basis. In the event of extreme load, it may affect other customers, but rest assured that Shodo has sufficient server resources.
If you would like a dedicated server, please consider Shodo's Enterprise Plan.

What happens if I cancel during the contract?

If you cancel during the contract period, you can continue to use the service for the remaining contract period.
At the end of the contract period, the service will be changed to the Basic Plan and you will no longer be able to use the features and data provided in the paid plan.

For more details, please check the fees and payment details .

What happens if I change my paid plan or the number of contracted users during my contract?

If you change from a paid plan to another paid plan or change the number of contracted users, the plan change will be processed immediately.
Whether you upgrade or downgrade, your payment will be adjusted on the next payment date, so no additional payment or refund will be incurred on the change date.

For more details, please check the fees and payment details .

What happens if I change the contract period from monthly to annual?

The contract will be changed immediately, and the contract cycle and payment date will also change.
If additional payments are required, they will be required at the time of the change. If you have any remaining payments, such as when changing from annual to monthly, they will be carried over to the next contract renewal date.

For more details, please check the fees and payment details .

What happens if the number of people in a business plan increases or decreases?

With the Business plan, you can invite accounts to your organization up to the number of people you have contracted with.
If you reduce the number of people in your contract and there are too many accounts in the organization, Shodo will automatically remove them from the organization and adjust the number of people.

For more details, please check the fees and payment details .

What is your refund policy?

If you cancel your contract, there will be no refunds because you can use the service until the end of the contract period.
Also, if you delete a subscribed organization while there is still time left in the contract period, there will be no refunds.
In the event of a system trouble or other problem with Shodo, we may offer a refund as a guarantee to our customers.

For more details, please check the fees and payment details .

What is the difference between the Proofreading API Premium/Business Plan and the API Plan?

The functionality is the same. However, we ask that only contracted members use the Premium Business Plan. For example, use it to write blogs or books, or proofread internal documents.
The API Plan has no restrictions on users, and can be used by many people inside and outside the company. For example, use it to incorporate into services provided by customers, or to link with internal organization systems.

For more information about each service, please check the fees and payment details .
